Definition of Plant Diseases and Pests Detection Problem

Plant diseases and pests is one kind of natural disasters that affect the normal growth of plants and even cause plant death during the whole growth process of plants from seed development to seedling and to seedling growth. In machine vision tasks, plant diseases and pests tend to be the concepts of human experience rather than a purely mathematical definition.

To use our service, simply upload an image of a plant leaf or fruit showing signs of disease, and our system will analyze the image and provide you with a prediction of the likely disease.

How It Works

  1. Upload an image of a plant leaf or fruit showing signs of disease.
  2. Our system will analyze the image using machine learning algorithms.
  3. We'll provide you with a prediction of the likely disease affecting your plant.
  4. Take appropriate action based on the diagnosis to protect your plants and crops.

Our system is continuously learning and improving, thanks to user feedback and ongoing updates to our machine learning models.
